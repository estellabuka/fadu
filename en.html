Freewill Aid Development of Ukraine

On February 24, 2022, our lives changed.

War wants to take away our home - Ukraine. Many people will not return home today. The more of us, the stronger we are.

The greatest desire today is to stay alive and help the homeland to be bright, loved, and free again. We are people who are not indifferent to the future of Ukraine. Having gathered all our efforts, we created the Fund of Voluntary Assistance for the Development of Ukraine. We are the ones who stayed in our hometown - Kharkiv, to bring our victory closer together as soon as possible. Let's win together!

Every help is a saved life!
